# Cheatsheet

When you launch the game, you’ll be prompted to enter your name and the starting energy level you have (1-100). From there, your story will unfold through your decisions made throughout the day. At each prompt, you will type A or B and press Enter to make your decision.

The available commands are:
A: Choose option A at any prompt
B: Choose option B at any prompt
These are case-insensitive, and both “a” and “A”/ “b” and “B” work. If you type anything else, the game will ask you to try again from the start.

The game takes place across Smith College campus. As a player, you will move through 4 locations over the course of a single school day.

### [The Quad (your dorm)] → [Seelye Hall] → [Cutter/Ziskind Dining Hall] 

- Cutter/Ziskind Dining Hall (CZ)
    - Located at 10 Prospect St, Northampton, MA
    - Visit 1 (morning): Choose your tragic breakfast
    - Visit 2 (evening): Dinner with your group + the green tea ice cream moment

- The Quad
    - Your dorm/home base between commitments
    - A rabid cat ambush happens on your walk to class
    - After your morning class, you return here with a choice: grab food or take a nap

- Seelye Hall (Academic Building)
    - Located deep in the academic quad
    - Morning class: take notes or sleep
    - Afternoon class: group project with Stoner, Gunther, and Err
    - Merge conflict crisis — how will you resolve it?

- [Start / Main Menu]
    -  Type A to Play
    - Type B to Quit
    - After a run ends, you can choose to replay

### Spoilers:

There are three ways to lose and one way to win.

In interaction 2, if you choose A (run for your life) then you get bitten and end up at the Schacht center and have to recover all day. Game over.

In Interaction 4, if you choose B (sit next to Gunther), then nothing gets done. You discover a Proofs assignment due at 11:59 PM and spend the night on it. Game over.

In interactions 5 and 6, if you choose A (ignore the merge conflict) AND A (open the ice cream) you won’t get the ice cream if you didn’t resolve your problems first. You lose.

The restricted path happens when in Interaction 5, you choose A (ignore the merge conflict), which makes you unable to choose option A in Interaction 6. Players who did resolve the conflict (B) during interaction 5 will be able to choose option A in Interaction 6. 

### To reach the best ending: 

- Hide behind the Smith sign from the cat (Interaction 2: B)
- Sit next to Stoner for the group project (Interaction 4: A)
- Resolve the merge conflict (Interaction 5: B)
- Open the freezer with no hesitation (Interaction 6: A)
- The ending: You squeal in glory at the sight of TWO FULL TUBS of green tea ice cream.
